Hospital Name

The hospital is the brain child of Dr. Amit Mehta, MD, Medicine (AIIMS). After studying and gaining work experience from premier institutes of India like AIIMS, New Delhi and PGIMER, Chandigarh, Dr. Mehta decided to come back to his native place Hisar. He joined Jindal Hospital and worked there tirelessly for nine years. In November 2002, under the able guidance of Dr. Mehta, we made a humble beginning as a 20 bedded hospital with the sole mission of providing “Care and Cure for Whole Family under One Roof”. Since its inception we have tried to remain at par with top institutions of the country in facilitating cost efficacious medical services to the patients without compromising the quality of care..
